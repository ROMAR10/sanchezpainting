About Us

We are fully bonded and insured!

We provide great services and ideas

If you have reached a point where you want to give your property the update it deserves, you can rely on our house painting experts for a quality and cost-friendly service. Serving the residents around the area of Houston for the past ten-plus years, our company has become one of the leading painting contractors in the area. We are dedicated to excellence, stand behind quality workmanship, and offer on-budget property restoration and update solutions to all our clients.

Our company is well-known for its interior painting and exterior paint job solutions. We excel at painting kitchen cabinets, wall painting, as well as various exterior painting projects. Other than that, we are available for any power washing, roof repairs and services, water damage repair and restoration, electrical lighting, etc. We operate with attention to the every detail our clients have in mind. We stand out with our easy access to top-shelf equipment and meticulous operation practices that guarantee on-time and on-budget project results. We guarantee your complete satisfaction.
